Content

Home page

Welcome to Triple Threat!! Here we tackle 3 elements of enjoying a
relaxing evening with your family, friends, co-workers, and other group members.
Have you ever made plans to attend a certain restaurant and upon arrival learn
of an unexpected wait time? On this website you will be able to navigate and plan
accordingly so that you can have an enjoyable evening/meal with your group.

Images for the Home page

[Page 2]

Here are the 3 things you can use on our website to better plan your evening:

  1. - Reservation! Instead of waiting to speak to someone over the phone,
    you can click on the reservation tab at the restaurant of your choosing
    and see what seating is available and would fit a group of your size.
    Click to reserve and experience a quick seating upon your arrival.
  2. - Order ahead! See the full menu of the restaurant of your choosing
    and order ahead of time. Enjoy a nice meal at the time you are seated.
  3. - What Restaurants are busy? Restaurants in your local area on this
    website will be color coded according to the number of reservations.
    Green will show a not so busy restaurant, Yellow will be moderately
    busy, and Red will be very busy. This will help you in your decision
    making of which restaurant to attend.

Images for the Page 2

[Page 3]

Navigating this site is simple. First create a username and password to create an account.
This will make it easier for you as the user and the restaurant to see the same user.
This will also provide you as the user the ability to save your payment information to save
you extra time as well. Whether on our app or on our website you will be asked to allow
Triple Threat to see your location in order to give local restaurants near you.

Triple Threat covers more than just one restaurant. It gives you multiple options to choose from.
Many restaurants have loved partnering with Triple Threat because it saves their employees time from
taking reservations over the phone and gives them plenty of time to have each reservations food
prepared in a timely manner. Triple Threat has given an easier way of enjoying a stress free evening out.

WARNING!! Know that this is a first come, first serve experience! Those that reserve ahead of time for their
desired restaurant and seating arrangement will be expected to be there at the time reserved.
To avoid cancelations and inconveniencing the restaurant, you as the user will be expected to pay a
$10 deposit for the time slot selected. If your party is running late and arrive 15 minutes later than
the scheduled reservation, you will forfeit your deposit as well as your reservation so that the restaurant
can make room for the next reservation.
